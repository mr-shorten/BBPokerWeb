About

Welcome to the unofficial website of the poker games played at the Broken Blender Bar in downtown Anchorage, Alaska.

Our games are held every Monday and Thursday evening, starting promptly at 6:30 pm. Players compete for a Qualifier Ticket to the prestigious Annual Fur Rondy Tournament, with the opportunity to win big and earn bragging rights among the local poker community.

Each player starts with a stack of 5000 chips, with an additional 1000 chips added to your stack if you arrive on time at 6:30 pm. If you purchase any food item from the bar, you'll also receive a 4000 chip bonus to give you an even greater advantage at the table.

Our experienced dealers provide a welcoming and fun environment for both seasoned players and beginners alike. So whether you're looking to sharpen your poker skills or just want to have a great time with friends, come join us at the Broken Blender Bar for an unforgettable night of poker and camaraderie.
